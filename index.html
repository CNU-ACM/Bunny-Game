Welcome!

The Plain Green Tees was created by CNU students S.Darling, G. McAlister, J. Peyton, and J. Vallejo, inspired by top down graphics and MMO's interface. Susannah Darling designed the graphics, Gerald McAlister was the front end creater, Joseph Peyton did the web development, and Juan Vallejo did the technical backend. Instead of browsing through links, the Plain White Tees' game lets you browse by interacting with the game.
