about us

The Plain Green Tees was created by CNU students S.Darling, G. McAlister, J. Peyton, and J. Vallejo, inspired by top down graphics and MMO's interface. Susannah Darling designed the graphics, Gerald McAlister was the front end creater and team lead, Joseph Peyton did the web development, and Juan Vallejo did the technical backend. Instead of browsing through links, the Plain Green Tees game lets you browse by interacting with the game.

The valuable lessons we learned from this project are: collaborating across our individual parts of the project, learning when to concede based on other peopleâ€™s feedback, putting opinions and pride aside to move the project forward and the importance of source management.

The game features include:
- Hand drawn graphics
- Ability to interact with the world in the game
- Ability to browse products within the app
- Support for mobile through appcelerator
- Future proof through HTML5 and Javascript
- Facebook integration for authentication
